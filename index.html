Description of Machine

Mini crane is designed and manufactured as per the Indian site conditions. This crane can be assembled or dismantled in 1 hour with 2 labors.

The main unit of the crane consists of one motor with a gear train. The motor is lifted with fail safe electromagnetic brake. The machine is provided with a wire rope drum which is driven by the step motor through the gear train.

The control panel is fitted on the machine itself for motor operation.

Structure

  • Structural frame: fabricated from high tensile steel square rod.
  • Maximum strength of wire rope is 3700kg
  • Maximum speed 0.12 meter per minute.
  • Brakes are electromagnetic.
  • Maximum weight of machine 275kg.
  • Slew system, ball bearing swing circle with 360 degree continuous rotation.
Applications

Lifting cement bags, bricks, blocks, sand, metal chips, steel rebar, centering plate and any construction site material. Also very useful in mining or industries. The warehouse companies, for material handling companies.

Safety Features

  • Limit switch provided at the bottom of boom to cut the electricity if UP button pressed for a long time to avoid the accident.
  • Single phase preventer provided in the control panel.
  • Overload relay provided at the control panel.

Special Features

  • Maximum lifting capacity of 500kg at 4.5 feet radius.
  • 3 HP Crompton motor.
  • 8 mm wire rope Usha martin makes.
  • Maximum lifting height is 75m.
